Welcome to my homepage!

I'm a fifth-year Ph.D. student in Computational and Applied Mathematics at the University of Chicago, advised by Prof. Daniel Sanz-Alonso. Before coming to Chicago, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from UCLA in 2017. Broadly speaking, my research focuses on applications of graph-based and Gaussian process methods in statistical inverse problems and their asymptotic properties. I also work with Prof. Bryon Aragam on nonparametric mixture models.

I will be on the job market in the academic year 2021-2022.
